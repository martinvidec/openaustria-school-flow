## Decisions Made

- **Conditional $transaction (not unconditional):** Only wrap create() in $transaction when isActive === true. The isActive:false|undefined path stays direct — no wasted transaction overhead on the common case (schools create inactive years throughout the year, activate one at a time). This matches the behavioral test split (Test 1b vs Test 1c).
- **buildData() as local closure:** Keeps complex data-shape DRY across direct + transactional paths without surfacing a private method on the class. One reader, no external callers, no reason to promote.
- **API GET for single-active invariant, not DOM count:** SchoolYearCard renders both 'Aktiv' badge and 'Aktivieren' button (substring overlap noted in Phase 10.2-03 decisions). API layer is the canonical source of truth and resistant to UI refactors.
- **afterEach activate-first-then-delete:** SchoolYearService.remove throws ConflictException on active years (Test 4b). Without the compensating activate call on a non-throwaway seed year, afterEach would fail on the first throwaway and leak all others. Best-effort guards (yearsRes.ok()) absorb stack-down scenarios.
