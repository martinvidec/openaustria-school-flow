## Decisions Made

See `key-decisions` frontmatter above. In short:

- **No production code changes** — the 5 invariants already held; Plan 10.1-01 is effectively a regression-harness plan, not a bug-fix plan. This is an important finding for Plans 10.1-02 and 10.1-03: if a green toast still appears during manual UAT on a 4xx response, the cause is NOT in the 4 audited files and a different hook/component must be implicated (likely outside Phase 10 scope, e.g. a shared form handler or a Stammdaten-specific mutation we did not audit because it was out of scope).
- **OptionsTab coverage approach** — appended rather than split into a second file, to avoid maintaining two `__tests__/OptionsTab.*` files with overlapping mock scaffolding.
- **Scope boundary enforcement** — 8 tsc errors in unrelated files (CreateSchoolYearDialog, useImportSocket, usePushSubscription, keycloak.ts, socket.ts, main.tsx, 2 route files) triaged to `deferred-items.md` per executor Scope Boundary rule. A dedicated `web-typecheck-gap-closure` mini-plan is recommended (~1–2h; mostly `vite-env.d.ts` additions + one zod-resolver generic narrowing).
